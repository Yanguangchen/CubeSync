How the RPA Bot Queue Works

Which forms appear here?
Only forms with status Ready (set in the staff dashboard) appear in this queue. Draft and Archived forms are excluded.
How are forms sorted by date?
Forms are grouped by the date they were marked Ready (submittedAt), falling back to their creation date. All times are in Singapore timezone (SGT).
What do the ERP statuses mean?
  • Pending — Waiting for the bot to pick up.
  • Processing — Bot is entering data into ERP. RPA status changes to "In Progress".
  • Success — Data entered successfully. RPA status changes to "Submitted to ERP".
  • Error — Bot encountered an issue. RPA status changes to "Failed".
What does Disable RPA do?
Disabling a form removes it from bot processing and CSV exports. The form remains in Firestore but the bot will skip it. Click "Enable RPA" to re-activate it.
Export all CSV
Exports all enabled (non-disabled) Ready forms as a ZIP of individual CSV files, one per form. Disabled forms are excluded from the export.
Navigating dates
Use "Previous Day" or the date picker to view past queues. The "Today" button returns to the current SGT date. Future dates are not selectable.
