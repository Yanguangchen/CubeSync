Welcome to CubeSync

Concrete cube test requests, done digitally.

CubeSync replaces the paper cube request form for R.A.K. Materials Consultants. This quick tour shows you how to submit a request, how staff review them, and what happens after. It takes about three minutes.

2 Submit a request

For customers and site staff. The Digital form is the easiest way in.

  1. Open the Digital formFrom the home page or the top navigation.
  2. Fill the required fieldsCustomer (Billing), Contact, Grade, Supplier, Location, Date of cast, slumps, Size, and the Persons in charge. The live form marks exactly what's required.
  3. Use the autocomplete suggestionsStart typing and pick a match. Typing something new is allowed — it just gets flagged for staff to review.
  4. Add at least one test-result rowSet number, size, specimen reference and dates. A barcode is generated automatically.
  5. Complete the “I'm not a robot” checkThis protects the system from spam submissions.
  6. Click SaveYour request is stored as a Draft for staff to review. Done!
⚠️
If the form won't submit, it jumps back to the first empty required field and highlights it. Fill that field and try again.

3 What happens next

Every request moves through three states. Only staff can move it forward.

Step 1

Draft

Every new submission lands here automatically. It's waiting for staff to review.

Step 2

Ready

Staff approve it. Only Ready requests enter the RPA/ERP queue for processing.

Step 3

Archived

Completed or set-aside requests, tucked out of the active working list.

4 Review on the dashboard

For staff. Sign in with an approved Google account to manage submissions.

🔍 Search & filter

Filter the list, sort columns, and flip on Today only to focus on today's work.

✏️ Edit & save

Open a request, change any field, and save — only what you changed is written back.

🟠 Free-text review

Values typed instead of picked are tinted orange with a badge, so you can verify customer entries.

✅ Set status

Promote to Ready or move to Archived. Print a request as a single A4 sheet.

🧩
Setting a request to Ready also promotes any new free-text values into the shared dropdown lists, so everyone gets them as suggestions next time.

5 Configure the forms

Staff can tailor the public forms from Dashboard → Field settings — no code required.

You want to…Do this
Show/hide a fieldToggle request fields or result columns on/off in Field settings.
Rename a field for customersEdit its label — this changes only what customers see; internal data stays the same.
Add a new fieldAdd a custom request field (text, number, date, checkbox or textarea) and enable it.
Update dropdown suggestionsOpen Manage options to edit the shared autocomplete lists for everyone.

6 If something goes wrong

The most common bumps and how to clear them.

SymptomWhat to do
“reCAPTCHA is still loading”Wait a moment and refresh the page, then submit again.
“reCAPTCHA verification failed”Re-tick I'm not a robot and submit again.
Can't sign in to the dashboardOnly approved staff have access. Ask an admin to add your Google email.
Form jumps to a fieldThat required field is empty or invalid — fill it and resubmit.
Dropdowns look emptyReport it to an administrator; the option lists may need attention.
Old version keeps showingCubeSync caches for offline use — hard-refresh the page or reinstall the app.
📶
CubeSync works offline as an installable app: the forms load without a connection, though signing in and saving still need internet. Install it from your browser's menu (Install app / Add to Home Screen).
